FAQs

What is included in the ticket price?

More than 240 wines rated 90+ for you to taste (red, white, rosé, sparkling and dessert), a beautiful souvenir Riedel™ wine glass, and a delicious selection of food to enjoy.

 

What is included in the VIP Ticket?

Everything outlined above plus an extra hour to taste.

 

What time does the Grand Tour start?

The event runs from 6:00-10:00 PM with a VIP hour from 6:00-7:00 PM

 

Is there a tasting map of the event?

Yes, tasting maps will be available to view and print closer to the event. Check back!

 

Do I need to print my ticket?

Yes, we suggest that you print your ticket. However, if you have access to your ticket on your mobile device, we can also scan the ticket from there.
