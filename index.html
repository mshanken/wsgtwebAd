FAQs

What is included in the ticket price?

200 wines rated 90+ for you to taste (red, white, rosé, sparkling and dessert), a beautiful souvenir Riedel™ wine glass, and a delicious selection of food to enjoy.

 

What is included in the VIP Ticket?

Everything outlined above plus an extra hour to taste.

 

What time does the Grand Tour start?

The event runs from 6:00-10:00 PM with a VIP hour from 6:00-7:00 PM.

 

Is there a tasting map of the event?

Yes, tasting maps will be available to view and print closer to the event. Check back!

 

What is the covid policy for the event?

All ticketed attendees agree to comply with all current Grand Tour, county and venue health & safety protocols and/or mandates at the time of the event. Grand Tour reserves the right to refuse entry or remove an individual from the event if any such protocols are breached.
