Q. What is included in the ticket price?

 

A. More than 200 wines rated 90+ for you to taste (red, white, rose, sparkling and dessert), a beautiful souvenir Riedel™ wine glass, and a delicious selection of food to enjoy.

 

Q. What time does the Grand Tour start?

 

A. 6:00-7:00 PM - VIP Entry

7:00-10:00 PM - General Admission

10:00 PM - Event Ends

 

Q. What do I need at the door?

 

A. Please remember to bring your ticket. Tickets can be printed out (recommended) or scanned on your mobile device. If you need help finding your ticket please contact eTix at 1-919-653-0444 or e-mail support@etix.com.

 

Additional Questions?
