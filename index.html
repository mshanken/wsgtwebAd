FAQs

What is included in the ticket price?

More than 200 wines rated 90+ for you to taste (red, white, rosé, sparkling and dessert), a beautiful souvenir Riedel™ wine glass, and a delicious selection of food to enjoy.

 

What is included in the VIP Ticket?

Everything outlined above plus an extra hour to taste.

 

What time does the Grand Tour start?

The event runs from 6:00-10:00 PM with a VIP hour from 6:00-7:00 PM.

 

Is there a tasting map of the event?

Yes, tasting maps will be available to view and print closer to the event. Check back!

 

What is the refund policy?

The 2020 Grand Tour has been cancelled. Once the 2021 event dates have been announced, ticket buyers will recieve an email with more information about how to request a refund if the new 2021 dates do no work for them. If you purchased tickets to the 2020 event, please check your email for more information from 'Wine Spectator's Grand Tour'.

 

What safety precautions will the Grand Tour and its host venues be taking due to COVID-19 concerns?

We are working closely with our host venues and internally to implement health and safety protocols that will ensure a safe and enjoyable experience. We will be sharing an overview of those protocols once they are finalized. Stay tuned!
